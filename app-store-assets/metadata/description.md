Your Date Genie is the AI date planner built for couples who are done with indecision.

Tell us the vibe — romantic, adventurous, cozy. Set the budget. Pick the city. In 60 seconds you'll have a custom date plan: where to go, when to be there, what to order, and what to do next. No swiping, no scrolling endless lists, no arguing about Yelp reviews.

ROMANCE, REIMAGINED

We built Your Date Genie because the answer to "what are we doing tonight?" shouldn't take an hour. The app generates personalized plans grounded in your vibe, your budget, your dietary needs, and the season. Every plan is curated, never random.

WHAT YOU GET FREE

• Three custom date plans per month
• All major US cities, starting with NYC
• Vibe selector — romantic, adventurous, cozy, foodie, low-key
• Budget filter — $, $$, $$$
• Dietary preferences — vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, halal, kosher
• Save your plans to revisit

PREMIUM ($14.99/month or $99/year)

• Unlimited date plans
• Memories — save plans with photos and notes
• Partner Planning — collaborative editing with your significant other
• Seasonal packs — Valentine's, anniversary, holiday, Pride, summer
• Priority customer support

PERFECT FOR

• New couples who want to make every date count
• Long-term partners refilling the well after kids, work, life
• Solo daters planning a third or fourth date with someone special
• Anyone who's tired of "I don't know, where do YOU want to go?"

BUILT IN NEW YORK

We started in NYC because no city tests a dating app like ours does. From Bar Penny in the West Village to that one quiet bench on the Hudson — every venue in our system has been vetted by real couples. We expand to LA, Chicago, Austin, and Miami throughout 2026.

ON PRIVACY

Your dates are yours. We never sell your data. We don't track you across apps. We don't share who you're dating with anyone. Plans you save are encrypted and visible only to you and the partner you invite.

ON THE AI

Your Date Genie uses GPT-4o to generate plans, but the venue list is human-curated and the recommendations are vetted by an editorial team that cares as much about a good wine list as the algorithm does. Every plan card carries a "verify hours and prices" reminder — we work hard to keep info fresh, but venues change and we want you to walk in confident.

Romance, Reimagined.

## Notes on this description

- Opens with the hook in one line — Apple shows only the first 3 lines before the "more" tap.
- Free vs. Premium feature lists in plain bullets — App Store reviewers verify these match what the paywall actually offers.
- "Built in New York" is the brand differentiator. Don't bury it.
- The privacy paragraph is intentionally specific — App Store Connect reviewers cross-check against your Privacy Nutrition Label.
- The AI paragraph names the model (4o) and the editorial backstop — this answers Apple's §4.0 / §5.1 questions about AI-generated content before they get asked.
- "verify hours and prices" disclaimer is intentional — it's the same copy that needs to appear on every plan card in the app.
