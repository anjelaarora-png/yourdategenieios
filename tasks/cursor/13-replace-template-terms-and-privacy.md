## Task breakdown

### Step 1 — Convert Terms markdown to React/JSX

Read `legal/terms-of-service-draft.md` (already updated to LLC).

Replace `src/pages/Terms.tsx` (or wherever the `/terms` page renders) with a structured React component. Don't paste the entire markdown as one giant string — use proper headings, sections, and Tailwind typography classes.

Recommended structure:

```tsx
import { useEffect } from 'react'
import { Link } from 'react-router-dom'

export default function TermsOfService() {
  useEffect(() => {
    document.title = 'Terms of Use | Your Date Genie'
  }, [])

  return (
    <article className="prose prose-lg mx-auto max-w-3xl px-6 py-12">
      <h1>Terms of Use</h1>
      <p className="text-sm text-gray-500">Effective: May 27, 2026 · Last updated: May 27, 2026</p>

      <h2>1. About these Terms</h2>
      <p>
        Welcome to Your Date Genie. These Terms of Use ("Terms") form a legally binding
        agreement between you and Your Date Genie LLC ("Date Genie," "we," "us," or "our")…
      </p>

      {/* …all sections from the markdown draft, structured as <h2>, <h3>, <p>, <ul>… */}

      <h2>14. Contact</h2>
      <p>
        Questions about these Terms? Email us at{' '}
        <a href="mailto:hello@yourdategenie.com">hello@yourdategenie.com</a>.
      </p>

      <p className="mt-12 text-sm text-gray-500">
        Your Date Genie LLC. All rights reserved.
      </p>
    </article>
  )
}
```

If the project uses a markdown renderer (e.g. `react-markdown`), you can render the markdown directly:

```tsx
import ReactMarkdown from 'react-markdown'
import termsContent from '../../legal/terms-of-service-draft.md?raw'

export default function TermsOfService() {
  return (
    <article className="prose prose-lg mx-auto max-w-3xl px-6 py-12">
      <ReactMarkdown>{termsContent}</ReactMarkdown>
    </article>
  )
}
```

This keeps a single source of truth (the .md file) — when lawyer makes edits, only one place to update.

If most are missing (likely), draft a new version. Cursor should generate a draft based on this template:

```markdown
# Privacy Policy

Effective: May 27, 2026

## Who we are
Your Date Genie LLC ("Date Genie," "we," "us") operates the Your Date Genie app and yourdategenie.com.

## What information we collect
- Account information you provide: name, email, partner email, location preferences, relationship preferences.
- Information generated by your use of the app: date plan history, saved plans, love notes you create.
- Device information: app version, device type, OS version, crash logs.
- Usage analytics on our website (collected via Plausible — privacy-friendly, no cookies).

## How we use it
- To create personalized AI date plans for you.
- To send you transactional emails (welcome, plan reminders).
- To improve the product (in aggregate, never individually identifiable).

## Third-party services we use
- **Supabase** (database + auth) — hosts your account data securely
- **OpenAI** (AI generation) — your prompts (date preferences, love note text) are sent to OpenAI to generate suggestions. We do not send your name or email.
- **Apple App Store** — handles all payment processing for in-app subscriptions
- **Resend** (email delivery) — sends transactional emails from us to you
- **Firebase** (waitlist only) — pre-launch signups; data is migrated and archived after launch

## Your rights
Under GDPR (EU) and CCPA (California), you have the right to:
- Access your data (in-app: Settings → Export My Data)
- Delete your account and all associated data (in-app: Settings → Delete Account)
- Opt out of marketing emails (every marketing email has an unsubscribe link)
- Request a copy of all data we hold about you (email hello@yourdategenie.com)

## Data retention
- Active account data: retained for as long as your account is active
- Deleted accounts: purged within 30 days of deletion request
- Crash logs and analytics: aggregated and retained 90 days

## Children
Date Genie is not intended for users under 17. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

## International transfers
We process data in the United States. By using the app, you consent to data transfer to the U.S.

## Contact
Questions? Email hello@yourdategenie.com.
[CONFIRM: privacy@yourdategenie.com if alias is set up]

## Changes
We may update this policy. Material changes will be notified via in-app banner and email.

Your Date Genie LLC. All rights reserved.
```
